Welcome to LOFI LABS!

We make interactive audio NFTs.

Our first collection is called DHC- 1.
It is a 3D interactive loop & sample player with FX.
128 DHC-1 can now be minted on fxhash:

https://www.fxhash.xyz/generative/slug/dhc-1

Turn on audio & try the device:

CONTROLS:

* click & drag - focus & turn device
* scroll / mousewheel - zoom in & out
* click PAD 1 to start/stop loop
* click PAD 2 & 3 to trigger samples
* click FX buttons to toggle fx on/off for the according pad
* drag fader to adjust FX level (when turned on)

KEYS:

* A - PAD 1
* S - PAD 2
* D - PAD 3

* 1 - FX PAD 1
* 2 - FX PAD 2
* 3 - FX PAD 3
